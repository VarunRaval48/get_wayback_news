Cast Announced for Come Fly Away

  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
Compiled by JULIE BLOOM
Published: January 17, 2010

Audiences can now prepare for boarding: “Come Fly Away” has its crew. The new Broadway musical conceived, choreographed and directed by Twyla Tharp and based on the music of Frank Sinatra, has announced its cast. The show, about four couples who fall in and out of love at a nightclub, stars Matthew Dibble, Holley Farmer, Laura Mead, Charlie Neshyba-Hodges, Rika Okamoto, Karine Plantadit, Keith Roberts and John Selya, all of whom appeared in last fall’s production of the show, then called “Come Fly With Me,” at the Alliance Theater in Atlanta and at least two of whom appeared in Ms. Tharp’s 2002 Broadway hit “Movin’ Out” (that one was based on songs by Billy Joel). The show is to begin previews on March 1 and open on March 25 at the Marquis Theater.
