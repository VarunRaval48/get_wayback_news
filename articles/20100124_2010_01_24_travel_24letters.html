Letter: Antarctica's Allure

  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
  • Reprints
Published: January 24, 2010

To the editor: Great new, enticing picks in “The 31 Places to Go in 2010” (Jan. 10) to whet the appetite for unusual travel. However, as a many-time traveler to Antarctica since the early 1980s, the statements suggesting that tourist travel has been only recent belie the facts. While 2010 may be a good year to go, ships complying with new standards at a later date will continue to make this continent the ultimate destination for those who have a love of unsurpassed natural beauty.

Joan Larsen
Park Forest, Ill.
