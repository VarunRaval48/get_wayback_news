What You Get for ... $250,000

Properties for $250,000

WHAT: A two-bedroom two-bath town house built in 1852

HOW MUCH: $249,000

SIZE: 2,246 square feet

PER SQUARE FOOT: $110.86

SETTING: This house is in a residential area of mostly antebellum homes about two blocks from downtown Natchez. Natchez, a city of about 19,000, is on the Mississippi River.

INSIDE: The lower level of this home, currently used as law offices, has a reception room, a kitchenette and a study with floor-to-ceiling bookshelves. On the upper level is a dining room (with French doors opening to a porch), a kitchen (opening to a garden), a living room and two bedrooms. Mantels, hardwood floors and exposed brick are original to the home. The house was renovated in 2006.

OUTDOOR SPACE: There is a porch off the dining room. A private garden off the kitchen has a stone patio and a fountain.

TAXES: $674 a year

CONTACT: Ernesto Caldeira, Dorian Bennett Sotheby’s International Realty, (504) 450-8991; ernestocaldeira.com

SONOITA, ARIZ.

WHAT: A one-bedroom one-bath house on two acres

HOW MUCH: $247,000

SIZE: 817 square feet

PER SQUARE FOOT: $302.33

SETTING: Sonoita, population 830, is in a valley nearly 5,000 feet high, surrounded by mountain ranges. The town square, about five miles away, has several restaurants, a post office and a bank. Grocery shopping is about 35 miles away in Nogales to the southwest, Sierra Vista to the southeast, and Tucson to the north. A mile west is the 1.78-million-acre Coronado National Forest.

INSIDE: The house was built and situated to take advantage of the views, which are primarily to the west and southwest. The floors are brick pavers with radiant heat. The great room has three eight-by-eight-foot windows with mountain views. The bedroom has a walk-in closet with a study nook. The garage — about 400 square feet — is attached, heated and currently used as an art studio.

OUTDOOR SPACE: The house is on two acres of rolling grassland.

TAXES: $968 a year

CONTACT: Anne Gibson, Long Realty Sonoita/Patagonia (520) 490-8264; annegibson.com

ATLANTA

WHAT: A two-bedroom one-bath loft-style condo

HOW MUCH: $246,500

SIZE: 1,398 square feet

PER SQUARE FOOT: $176.32

SETTING: This condo is on the upper level of a two-story conversion that was originally built in 1920 as a department store. The neighborhood around it, Castleberry Hill, is a former industrial area consisting mostly of low-lying commercial buildings built along the railroad. A couple of blocks away are restaurants, bars and galleries. Downtown Atlanta is about a mile away.

INSIDE: The apartment is an open space with 16-foot ceilings and western exposure. The downstairs bedroom is currently used as an office. The lofted master bedroom is accessible by a spiral staircase. Under it, there’s a storage cubby. Off the living room is a Juliet balcony. There’s a laundry room next to the unit’s bathroom. The apartment comes with one parking space in the building’s garage, and a second spot in a gated outdoor lot.

OUTDOOR SPACE: A balcony off the living-room area, and a private roof deck.

TAXES: $2,792 a year; $245 a month in homeowner’s association dues

CONTACT: Marilou Young, Ian Marshall Realty, (770) 601-6950; ianmarshall.com
