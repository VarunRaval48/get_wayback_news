Quotation of the Week

Published: January 9, 2010
  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
  • Reprints

“Santa delivers the goods.”

Ken Perkins, president of the research firm Retail Metrics, after retailers as varied as Costco and Saks posted robust increases when compared with last season’s totals at stores open at least a year, a crucial measure of retail health known as same-store sales.
