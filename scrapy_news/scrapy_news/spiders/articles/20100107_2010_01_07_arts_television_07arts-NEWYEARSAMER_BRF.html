New Year, Same Ratings

  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
  • Reprints
By BENJAMIN TOFF; Compiled by DAVE ITZKOFF
Published: January 6, 2010

New episodes of “NCIS” and “NCIS: Los Angeles” helped lift CBS above its rivals on Tuesday night, even against the FedEx Orange Bowl on Fox and the season premiere of “The Biggest Loser” on NBC. According to Nielsen’s estimates, 21.3 million viewers tuned in to “NCIS,” and 18 million viewers watched its spinoff, “NCIS: Los Angeles.” Fox was likely to place second with its live football broadcast, for which final ratings were not immediately available, but NBC appeared to be in a close race with Fox. NBC earned an average of 11.7 million viewers for “The Biggest Loser: Couples,” nearly matching the series’ highest rated debut a year ago (11.9 million).
