Guineas Ruler Surfaces

  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
  • Reprints
By THE ASSOCIATED PRESS
Published: January 13, 2010

OUAGADOUGOU, Burkina Faso (AP) — The leader of Guinea’s junta, who had been getting medical treatment in Morocco, has traveled here to Burkina Faso, east of Guinea, for continued care.

The surprise move by the leader, Capt. Moussa Dadis Camara, came just as Guinea appeared to be making steps toward a return to civilian rule. Many people fear that Captain Camara — who was shot in the head by his aide-de-camp a little over a month ago — will return to Guinea soon or destabilize the country from exile.
