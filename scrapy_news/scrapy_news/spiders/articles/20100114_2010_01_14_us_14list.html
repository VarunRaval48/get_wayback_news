Names of the Dead

  • Sign In to E-Mail
  • Print
  • Reprints
Published: January 13, 2010

The Department of Defense has identified 941 American service members who have died as a part of the Afghan war and related operations. It confirmed the deaths of the following Americans Wednesday:

INGHAM, Matthew N., 25, Staff Sgt., Marines; Altoona, Pa.; Third Marine Division.

LOWE, Jamie R., 21, Cpl., Marines; Johnsonville, Ill.; Third Marine Division.

UZENSKI, Nicholas K., 21, Cpl., Marines; Tomball, Tex.; Third Marine Division.
